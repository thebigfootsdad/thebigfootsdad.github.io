The difference between a topic and an issue

What is where?
Why there?
Why care?

What is a topic?

A topic would be considered the 'subject' or what is being talked about.

What is an issue?

An issue is an important subject open to discussion and debate

Characteristics of an issue:

  • Generates concern
  • Involves interrelated aspects (relates or connects to many things/ideas/people)
  • Has complex causes/reasons
  • Has complex solutions
  • There is no right answer

Finding an issue in a topic

Example:

Today's weather is a topic:
How the weather affects or contributes to global warming is the issue
Another issue is an increase in natural disasters
