Ingredients

  1. enough strawberries to cover the top of the pie.
  2. one (1) cup white sugar
  3. half (1/2) cup all purpose flour
  4. one (1) cup shortening ( melted butter )
  5. one (1) teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. one (1) pack whipped cream
  7. two (2) eggs
  8. three (3) medium sized bananas
  9. three (3) tablespoons coconut shavings
  10. one and a half (1 1/2) cups chocolate chips
  11. two (2) tablespoons orange rind shavings
  12. 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  13. 1 tablespoon of lemon rind
  14. 1 9 inch pie crust
  15. 2 tablespoons of blackberry extract

Recipe:

  1. Mix sugar, eggs, and flour
  2. Stir in butter, lemon, cinnamon, coconut, orange, chocolate, and vanilla.
  3. Place banana slices on top of pie crust.
  4. Place mixture on top of bananas.
  5. Place pie pan on a cookie sheet.
  6. Bake in a preheated 165o C. oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes
  7. When out of the oven and cooled, spread the strawberries and whipped cream on top of the pie.
